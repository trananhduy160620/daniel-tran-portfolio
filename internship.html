My Internship Journey at Pick 'n' Clicks Design

12 weeks of growth, challenge, and creative exploration.

About the company

Pick 'n' Clicks Design is a dynamic and innovative web design studio that specializes in crafting modern, intuitive, and user-centered websites tailored for a diverse range of clients. During my time as a web design intern, I had the invaluable opportunity to immerse myself in real-world projects that challenged both my creativity and technical skills. From wireframing and prototyping to collaborating with developers and designers, I was actively involved in every step of the design process. I learned how thoughtful design can transform complex ideas into clean, engaging user experiences. This internship not only sharpened my attention to detail but also deepened my understanding of how great digital products are built—through collaboration, iteration, and a clear focus on the user.

My Role and Responsibility

Creating responsive layouts using HTML and Tailwind.

Designed wireframes and UI mockups using Figma.

Collaborated with developers to test and fix UI bugs.

Learned about accessibility and mobile-first design.

What I Learned

During this internship, I significantly enhanced my frontend development abilities while gaining a new perspective on how designers approach problems. It taught me to see beyond just code—to consider how every font choice, color palette, and layout decision affects user perception and interaction. I learned that good design isn’t just about making things look nice—it’s about creating meaningful, intuitive experiences. Understanding the balance between typography, color theory, and core UX principles helped me write code with intention, ensuring that every component not only functions well but also feels right to the user.

Projects I Contributed To

CoffeeBloom – A Coffee Shop Website Project

ReactJS, NodeJS

CoffeeBloom is a modern and responsive website designed for a cozy, local coffee shop aiming to build a stronger digital presence and attract new customers. The project focuses on creating an inviting user experience that reflects the warmth and charm of the café itself. Visitors can easily browse the full menu, learn about the shop’s story and values, and even reserve a table or place an order online. The design combines earthy tones, elegant typography, and high-quality imagery to evoke the aroma and atmosphere of freshly brewed coffee.
